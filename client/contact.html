FAQ

Frequently asked questions

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 3–5 business days. Express options are available at checkout for next-day or same-day delivery in select areas.

What is your return policy?

We offer a hassle-free 30-day return window. Items must be unworn and in their original packaging. Refunds are processed within 5–7 business days of receiving the return.

How do I book a personal styling session?

Fill out the contact form above or email us at styling@zovo.com. Our team will reach out within 24 hours to schedule your free 30-minute consultation.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes! We ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International delivery times vary from 7–14 business days depending on your location and selected shipping method.

How do I track my order?

Once your order is dispatched, you'll receive a confirmation email with a live tracking link so you can monitor your delivery every step of the way.
